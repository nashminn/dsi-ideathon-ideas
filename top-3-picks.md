# Top 3 Picks — DSI Ideathon 2026

Scored against weighted criteria: Business & Strategic Value (30%), Problem Clarity (25%), Originality (25%), Feasibility (20%).

---

## #1 — Idea 24: Microfinance Field Operations Agent

**Why:** Highest overall weighted score. No software incumbent — replacing a notebook, not a competitor. BD's MFI sector is massive, globally recognized, and structurally permanent. Loan history data accumulates into a compliance and fraud-detection asset that makes switching nearly impossible over time. Originality is high because no one has built this for the tier below BRAC/Grameen. Feasibility is clean — WhatsApp + Bangla text parsing + scheduling logic, no heavy inference.

**Strategic edge:** Data moat. Every month of loan collection history deepens lock-in and opens a fraud detection premium feature.

---

## #2 — Idea 25: School Fee Collection & Parent Communication Agent

**Why:** Clearest problem statement of all 27 ideas — every school owner understands the pain in 10 seconds. Fastest sales cycle in the pool. 100,000+ institutions, monthly recurring revenue, student database lock-in makes churn structurally low. Slightly lower originality than #24 but market size and problem clarity more than compensate.

**Strategic edge:** Volume. Even 1% market penetration at BDT 1,000/month is a BDT 1B/year business.

---

## #3 — Idea 22: SME Tax & VAT Compliance Agent

**Why:** The B2B2B channel through CA firms is the strategic differentiator — CA firms managing 50+ clients become high-value reseller accounts, solving distribution without a sales team. NBR is actively expanding the VAT net so the TAM grows passively. The compliance calendar recurs forever — the product re-sells itself every month when the deadline reminder lands.

**Strategic edge:** Growing regulatory tailwind. The government is doing the market expansion work for you.
