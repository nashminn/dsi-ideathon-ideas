# Top 3 Picks (v8) — DSI Ideathon 2026

Scored across all 50 ideas. Weighted criteria: Business & Strategic Value (30%), Problem Clarity (25%), Originality (25%), Feasibility (20%).

Filters applied: (1) primary user must not be able to game the agent and benefit; (2) buyer must be an enterprise — DSI cannot sell to fragmented individual consumers at scale; (3) idea must use DSI's AI stack meaningfully, not just arithmetic or scheduling; (4) market must be durable; (5) no DSI product collision.

---

## #1 — Idea 24: FieldLedger — Microfinance Collection Agent

**Why:** Strongest fraud-resistance mechanism in the pool. Two-sided audit trail that neither the officer nor the borrower can falsify alone. MFI management as enterprise buyer. MRA compliance pressure as regulatory tailwind. Data moat accumulates with every month of history. Global replication path (Kenya, Philippines, India) without rebuilding.

**Dishonesty resistance:** Officer logs payment → borrower receives receipt and must confirm → entry only commits on confirmation. Officer pocketing cash faces a borrower who got the wrong receipt amount. Neither party controls the record alone.

**DSI alignment:** Uses Bangla NLU (LLM reasoning over voice/text), WhatsApp Business API, LangGraph-style agentic flow. No incumbent software to displace — DSI enters a clean market.

---

## #2 — Idea 47: HajjReady — Hajj & Umrah Preparation Agent

**Why:** Highest originality score in the pool of 50. Nobody in a room of 300 engineers is submitting a Hajj preparation agent. The problem — families saving a decade for this trip, then failing at the airport over a preventable documentation error — is specific, high-stakes, and emotionally resonant in Bangladesh. Hajj agencies are clean enterprise buyers. Knowledge base is public and updates annually. Umrah operates year-round, making revenue continuous not seasonal.

**Dishonesty resistance:** A pilgrim who misreports their document status only misleads themselves — Saudi immigration verifies everything at the border.

**DSI alignment:** LLM reasoning over a structured preparation knowledge base, personalised timeline generation, multi-tenant white-labelling for agencies. Showcases contextual AI in a domain no competitor has touched.

---

## #3 — Idea 32: LoanReady — Bank Loan Preparation Agent

**Why:** Banks are DSI's natural enterprise clients. The non-obvious buyer insight (bank pays, not the applicant) shows business thinking the panel will respect. LLM multi-turn reasoning is the core differentiator — a PDF checklist cannot ask follow-up questions based on what the applicant already has. Zero API dependencies in MVP means no integration risk in the first build. Clean, demonstrable pitch scenario that any panel member can picture.

**Dishonesty resistance:** An applicant who lies about document readiness only hurts themselves — the bank verifies everything independently. No credit decision is made, no sensitive data stored.

**DSI alignment:** LLM multi-turn conversational reasoning over a structured bank-specific knowledge base. Sells to the same enterprise buyer type DSI already has relationships with. Internationally valid — same gap exists in every emerging market.
