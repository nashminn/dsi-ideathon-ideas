# Top 3 Picks (Final) — DSI Ideathon 2026

Scored across all 33 ideas. Weighted criteria: Business & Strategic Value (30%), Problem Clarity (25%), Originality (25%), Feasibility (20%). Adjusted for competition context: 300+ DSI engineers with domain knowledge across logistics (ODMS), education (IPEMIS), civil registration (OpenCRVS), and enterprise software security.

---

## #1 — Idea 24: Microfinance Field Operations Agent

**Why:** DSI has never served the microfinance sector — no engineer on ODMS, IPEMIS, or OpenCRVS thinks about MFI field officers and paper notebooks. Zero risk of collision with other submissions. Uses DSI's exact AI stack (LangGraph, multi-agent, Bangla NLU), making it technically credible to judges who know what DSI can build. Microfinance is global — pitch lands equally well in Dhaka, Nairobi, or Manila, which matters to a company with US and UK offices.

**Strategic edge:** No incumbent software to displace. Data moat deepens every month. Fraud detection becomes a premium feature over time.

---

## #2 — Idea 29: Construction Site Daily Reporting & Material Audit Agent

**Why:** Real estate and construction developers are enterprise clients with real budgets — fits DSI's sales motion. Not adjacent to any existing DSI product, so no collision risk. The anomaly detection angle (material usage vs. progress mismatch) is technically interesting enough to earn engineer respect in the room. Internationally scalable — the same pitch works in any developing market with a construction boom.

**Strategic edge:** Developer has no existing solution for ground-truth site visibility. The financial pain (pilferage, budget overruns) is immediate and quantifiable. Judges can visualize the ROI in one sentence.

---

## #3 — Idea 32: LoanReady — Bank Loan Application Preparation Agent

**Why:** Banks are enterprise clients — fits DSI's enterprise sales profile. Not adjacent to any existing DSI product. The non-obvious insight — bank as buyer, not the applicant — shows business thinking that DSI leadership will respect over a pure technical pitch. Clean, demonstrable interaction flow that works well in a 7-minute pitch.

**Strategic edge:** No BD-specific competitor. Knowledge base approach means no risky API dependencies. Bank partnership gives distribution without a sales team.
