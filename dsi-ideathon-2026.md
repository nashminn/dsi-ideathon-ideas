# DSI Ideathon 2026

**Organized by:** Dynamic Solution Innovators (DSI)
**Website:** https://ideathon.dsinnovators.com/

---

## Overview

An innovation competition inviting participants to pitch an AI agent idea that solves real problems. **No coding required.**

The ideathon seeks ideas addressing:
- Solving real-world problems and pain points
- Automating repetitive tasks to reduce workflow friction
- Creating novel AI-powered experiences that enhance work

## Eligibility

- Individual submissions only (no teams)
- Maximum 3 ideas per person
- All submissions must be in English
- Participants must agree to IP terms upon submission

---

## How to Submit (5 Questions)

1. What problem is being solved?
2. What is the AI agent concept?
3. Who is the beneficiary audience?
4. Why is the timing right for this?
5. What is the feasibility scope?

Ideas are tagged as either **"Quick Build"** or **"Big Bet"**.

## Competition Phases

1. **Submission Window** — Online form submissions with public tracking
2. **Committee Shortlisting** — Selection of top 10 ideas (anonymized review)
3. **Workshop** — Intensive 1-day mentorship with pitch deck development
4. **Grand Final** — Live 7-minute pitches with Q&A before DSI leadership

---

## Intellectual Property Terms

- Submitters grant DSI **full ownership** of submitted concepts
- Revenue potential: **1–5% of net profits** (capped at $50,000 lifetime)
- Ideas revert to submitter if development doesn't begin within **24 months**
- Participants **cannot independently pursue** submitted ideas during the 24-month window
