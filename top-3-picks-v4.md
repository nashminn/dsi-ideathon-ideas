# Top 3 Picks (Final v4) — DSI Ideathon 2026

Scored across all 36 ideas. Weighted criteria: Business & Strategic Value (30%), Problem Clarity (25%), Originality (25%), Feasibility (20%). Adjusted for competition context: 300+ DSI engineers with domain knowledge across logistics (ODMS), education (IPEMIS), civil registration (OpenCRVS), and enterprise software security.

---

## #1 — Idea 24: Microfinance Field Operations Agent

**Why:** DSI has never served the microfinance sector — zero collision risk with any existing DSI product team. Uses DSI's exact AI stack (LangGraph, multi-agent, Bangla NLU). No incumbent software to displace — replacing a paper notebook. Loan collection history accumulates into a fraud detection and compliance asset that competitors can't replicate from scratch. Microfinance is global — scales to Nairobi, Manila, or Dhaka without rebuilding.

**Strategic edge:** Deepest data moat of all 36 ideas. Every month of history increases switching cost and opens premium fraud detection and MRA compliance reporting features.

---

## #2 — Idea 35: GreenPass — ESG & Sustainability Compliance Agent for BD Exporters

**Why:** EU CSDDD enforcement in 2027 means international buyers are already contractually requiring ESG reports from BD suppliers — the market development is being done by European regulation, not by a sales team. DSI has existing RMG sector relationships, giving distribution on day one rather than cold outreach. No DSI product team works on factory compliance data collection. Internationally scalable to Vietnam, Cambodia, Sri Lanka, and any export-manufacturing economy facing the same buyer pressure.

**Strategic edge:** DSI-specific distribution advantage via existing RMG client relationships. Regulatory deadline creates urgency that makes the sale easier every quarter until 2027.

---

## #3 — Idea 32: LoanReady — Bank Loan Application Preparation Agent

**Why:** Banks are enterprise clients — fits DSI's enterprise sales profile. The non-obvious insight (bank as buyer, not the applicant) shows business thinking that DSI leadership will respect over a pure technical pitch. No BD-specific competitor. Clean, demonstrable 7-minute pitch scenario. Knowledge base approach means no risky API dependencies in the POC.

**Strategic edge:** Bank partnership gives distribution without a sales team. Internationally valid — same loan preparation gap exists in every emerging market.
