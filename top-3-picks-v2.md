# Top 3 Picks (Revised) — DSI Ideathon 2026

Scored across all 30 ideas. Weighted criteria: Business & Strategic Value (30%), Problem Clarity (25%), Originality (25%), Feasibility (20%).

---

## #1 — Idea 30: Legal Document Drafting Agent

**Why:** Highest margin product in the pool. Global proof of concept exists (LegalZoom is a multi-billion dollar company) but zero BD-specific competition. Near-zero marginal cost per document after templates are built. Three revenue streams: per-document fee, monthly subscription, law firm white-label accounts. One-time dependency — a BD lawyer reviews base templates once; after that, every generation is pure margin. Expands beyond BD without rebuilding anything.

**Strategic edge:** Globally validated model, locally uncontested. High-margin SaaS with a natural enterprise channel (law firms, CA firms) that sells itself.

---

## #2 — Idea 24: Microfinance Field Operations Agent

**Why:** Most defensible long-term position of all 30 ideas. No incumbent software to displace — replacing a paper notebook. BD's MFI sector is permanent, globally recognized, and politically protected. Loan collection history accumulates into a fraud detection and compliance asset that no late competitor can replicate from scratch. Highly original for the BD market.

**Strategic edge:** Data moat. Every month of history deepens lock-in and opens premium fraud detection and MRA compliance reporting features.

---

## #3 — Idea 25: School Fee Collection & Parent Communication Agent

**Why:** Highest problem clarity of all 30 — any school owner understands the pain in one sentence, making this the fastest path from pitch to paying customer. 100,000+ private schools and coaching centers, monthly recurring revenue, student database lock-in keeps churn structurally low. Simplest build in the top tier.

**Strategic edge:** Volume and speed. Fastest sales cycle, largest addressable institution count, lowest onboarding friction.
